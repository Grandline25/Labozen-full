About SECAN

The Society of Experimental and Clinical Anatomists of Nigeria (SECAN) is a leading professional and academic body dedicated to the advancement of anatomical sciences in Nigeria.

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized authority in anatomical sciences, driving innovation, research excellence, and clinical relevance in Nigeria and beyond.

Our Mission

To promote high-quality research in experimental and clinical anatomy and enhance teaching and learning of anatomy in medical institutions.

Core Objectives

Advancing knowledge in morphological sciences, encouraging translational research, supporting young scientists, and promoting ethical standards in anatomical research.
