Leading Anatomical Sciences Community

SECAN is a leading professional and academic body dedicated to advancing anatomical sciences in Nigeria. We bring together anatomists, biomedical scientists, educators, and clinicians committed to excellence in research, teaching, and the clinical application of anatomy. Our mission is to promote high-quality research and enhance medical education across Nigeria.
